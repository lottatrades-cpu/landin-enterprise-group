Steven Carreon Landin
Founder & Lead Consultant

Steven has 4+ years of experience in business consulting, entity formation, and capital strategy. Having personally founded and operated multiple LLCs across different industries, he brings practical, real-world insight to every client relationship. Born as a first-generation immigrant in Seattle, WA, Steven built his consulting career from the ground up, learning through experience what no one ever taught him: how to structure a business, access capital, and navigate a system not designed with newcomers in mind. He founded Landin Enterprise Group with one mission: to make sure other business owners never have to figure it out alone.
